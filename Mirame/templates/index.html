How Mirame works

A few steps to get yourself into the game!

  • Introduce yourself

    Register and log into your home page

  • The Real Deal

    Take a picture of your Gaming Rig, Desktop Workstation, Laptop setup at college or home, give a small description about the products which make up the setup, and post it onto Mirame.

  • Let the games begin!

    People who are registered or unregistered can vote and give their opinions for your setups.

  • May the most deserving ones win!

    Users(registered and unregistered) can vote for the most outstanding systems and rigs. The uploaded snapshots with the most votes wins amazing prizes and will stand out as the 'Techno-geek of the Week'
